University of South Carolina - CSCE 190 Project Assignment

For this assignment, I was required to find a problem that exists for students at the Universiy of South Carolina. After identifying a problem, the goal was to brainstorm an app that would solve such problem.

Highlighted Assignments

Problem Statement

Students at USC need a way to track how many meal swipes they have on an accessible, easy to use app.

Personas

I created 3 different personas for the people that would be using this feature if USC provided it.

Storyboards

I created 3 different stroyboards that depicts situations in which one might come to use this feature if USC provided it.

Sketches

I made a series of sketches of what the app will look like when it's being used in different ways.

Affinity Diagram

I created a cluster of topics that are integral to the functionality of the app, along with answers to possible questions that may arise.

Prototype

I created a short video of what way the average student might use this app.
